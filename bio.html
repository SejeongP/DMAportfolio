    About Me

    Sejeong Park is a first-year student at SJSU majoring in Digital Media Art. She was born in South Korea, and after she moved to the United States, she started creating art seriously in 2017. In the past four years, she used to work a lot with watercolor and gouache, but she decided to challenge her potential to learn a new medium of art. She is currently heading to what the world is going towards, the digital world.
