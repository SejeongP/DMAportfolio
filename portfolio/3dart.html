    2022 3D sculpture
    Mediums: 3 wood , chisels, disc sander, band saw, wood glue
    Size: 18x8x3.5 in

    In my 3D font project, I wanted to play with a homonym, which is a word that has more than one meaning. I had a list of homonyms that I thought would be interesting to make an artwork of: compact, lie, desert, and lead. I chose the word "lie" because it was interesting to me how a word can be so contradictory that being dishonest, as one of the definitions, can be written the same as the other definition, the imagery of lying on a soft surface, unconcerned, and peaceful. In the piece, I tried to make the surface of the wood like a sofa that one would want to take a nap on. Then, for the other meaning, I depicted being untruthful by adding imagery of Pinocchioâ€™s nose. Ultimately, I wanted the viewer to use the visuals of the materials to connect those identities to the narrative image.
